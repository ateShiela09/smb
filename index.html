About Me

I'm a graphic designer who loves creating clean, thoughtful designs. I specialize in branding, invitations, and journal layouts. I enjoy exploring new ideas and helping people bring their vision to life through design.

I believe good design comes from persistence, curiosity, and attention to detail. I'm always learning and excited to work on meaningful projects.

I am currently pursuing my studies as an Information Technology student. In addition to being a student, I am also a working mother. My age is not a hindrance to my education, even if it differs from that of most students today. I firmly believe in the saying, "It is never too late to learn something new," and I remain committed to continuous learning and self-improvement.
